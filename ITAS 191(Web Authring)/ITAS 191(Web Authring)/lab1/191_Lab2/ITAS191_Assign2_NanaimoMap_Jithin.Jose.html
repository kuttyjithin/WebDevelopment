Details about Places

Woodgrove Centre

It is the largest shopping center in Vancouver Island which is located in Harbour city. It consists of about 200 shops. I love this place because of my favorite shops such as KFC, Wirlessseve, Sports Chek, etc. Moreover,There is a Canadian store where we will get the household items and grocery for a cheap price. I also love to go to Walmart which is one of the biggest shopping centers in the world. We are able to get most of the items which are essential here. However, there is a restaurant named white spot where we are able to get all the wordly food things.

Beban Park

It is like a recreation center which provides indoor swimming facility,tennis fields, parks for dogs and football fields etc. This the place is one of my Favorite because I not only play soccer but also play tennis. Both these games are my favorites. There are also a huge amount of auditorium and big rooms to celebrate occasion and parties. Perhaps, The atmosphere and surroundings are beyond our imagination.

HareWood

This is the place where I live which has a beautiful neighborhood.All my friends who came from different country live near this place. However, this is near to my university.There is also a big old soccer field where all my friends come to play at the weekends. Since we live in a clustered community, we were able to understand multiculturalism as well as we were able to make a good relationship with the people who came from the different country.

VIU & NDSS

Vancouver Island University is one of the top universities in Canada. Most of the programs are available here. Moreover, There are a lot of facilities such as Gymnasium, Theatre, Hairdressing. NDSS(Nanaimo District Secondary school) is not only a secondary school but also as a tourist spot. Perhaps, it consists of an Aquatic center and Nanaimo playground. A lot of tournaments and leagues are held in this ground.

DownTown

This is the center of dance pubs, service center,restaurant, departure bay, and ferries,etc. I always go to this place whenever I am free. I have been working in downtown for 2 months. Every day, I enjoyed a new form of life in DownTown. There is a lot of tourist spots downtown. We are also able to go for shipping, fishing and aquatic centers where we are able to see a huge amount of fishes. Moreover, There is a lot of beaches where we can spend our free time and leisure time.
